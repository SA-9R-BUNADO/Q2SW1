Bobby (boo boo bear) He is one of my favorite characters from the show. He's kind, loving, and so much more and also the older brother of another one of my favorite characters: Ronnie Anne! He's a sweet guy and the best boyfriend to Lori - the eldest sister in the Loud family. He is the standard. He shows basic respect for everyone and has never done not one bad thing in the show. It really shows how a man was raised based on their actions. He was raised by his mom, together with his sister. We need more men like him in real life who can actually treat women right. Meet Bobby Santiago!
Ronnie Anne She is the sister of Bobby Santiago. She is known to be fierce and a mean girl but in reality, she's a really sweet kid who was misconcepted to be someone she isn't. She's kind, helpful, and a well rounded girl. She and her brother should have more recognition. They're often being shadowed off by their show partner. She's so much more than just Lincoln's (From the Loud family's) partner. She's a talented kid who deserves to be more than just a side character. Learn more about Ronnie Anne!
The Mcbrides, Howard and Harold♥ They are one of the kindest people I have seen in the show. Their care for not only their child, Clyde, but to other people as well is something we need more in our community. They are kind, giving, and selfless people who are willing to give up everything for their child, Clyde. They can be overprotective sometimes but it's all for a good cause. They want to keep their child safe which is every parents' aim for their children. They raise their kid fine and teach him good morality. Mcbrides!
Clyde Mcbride He has a very wide construct of imagination. He's an only child so his parents tend to overprotect him but it's all for a good cause. He's a really sweet child who cares about the environment a lot. He was raised in a good household with loving parents. He was taught good morals ever since then which explains why he's a big crowd favorite. He is full of love, and willing to give that love away to others because that's who he is as a person. Clyde Mcbride, a fan favorite♥
